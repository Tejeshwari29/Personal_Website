About BCA

BCA
BCA is a three-year undergraduate course in computer application. This degree gives good knowledge in areas related to computer science, the IT industry and networking. It is considered an alternative to an engineering degree and students from science and non-math backgrounds can pursue a BCA degree. During the programme, a student studies topics like programming languages, database management, fundamentals of computing, operating, web designing and networking. After completing the BCA degree, a student can get hired in various public and private sector firms.
About Interface
Interface College of Computer Applications (ICCA) is established in 2021 at Davangere, the fastest growing city of middle Karnataka. ICCA is focusing on educating students in computer science and converting IT students into employable candidates. The college offer both foundation courses, as well as specialization with Academic Essentials/Job Oriented training with hands on technical training.
